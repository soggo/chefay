CHEF AYOOLA SIGNATURE DISHES

GOURMET

Get ready to embark on a culinary journey like no other with Chef Ayoola's gourmet dishes. Each dish is a work of art, with carefully selected ingredients expertly prepared to create a perfect harmony of flavors.

CHICKEN JAMBONNETTE SERVED WITH A PORT REDUCTION AND CARAMELISED APPLES

The Chicken Jambonnette is a standout dish, with tender meat that is slow-cooked and delicately seasoned to perfection. The dish is further enhanced with a Port reduction and caramelized apples that add depth and sweetness to the dish.

SLOW COOKED VEAL BANQUETTE, SWEET POTATO MASH AND STEAMED VEGETABLES

The Slow Cooked Veal Banquette is a decadent dish that is sure to impress. The velvety texture of the veal is paired perfectly with creamy sweet potato mash and steamed vegetables that retain their natural crunch.

ROASTED DUCKLING WITH A SIDE OF RISOTTO

If you're looking for something more indulgent, the Roasted Duckling is the perfect choice. The crispy skin and succulent meat are served with a side of risotto that is cooked to perfection, making every bite an unforgettable experience.

LAMB TAGINE SERVED WITH SPICY VEGETABLE RICE

For an aromatic and flavorful experience, try the Lamb Tagine. The lamb is slow-cooked in a blend of spices, served with a side of spicy vegetable rice that is bursting with flavors.

CREAMY SEAFOOD LINGUINE & FISH MOUSSELINE

Seafood lovers, don't miss out on the Creamy Seafood Linguine and Fish Mousseline. The dish features fresh seafood expertly paired with perfectly cooked linguine. The creaminess of the dish is perfectly balanced by the lightness of the fish mouselline, creating a perfect harmony of flavors.

APPETISERS

Indulge in a variety of flavorful and unique dishes from Chef Ayoola's kitchen. With a focus on fresh ingredients and bold flavors to tease, each appetiser is sure to impress.

COCONUT CRUSTED KING PRAWNS

The Coconut Crusted King Prawns are a must-try. The perfectly cooked prawns are coated with a delicate coconut crust that adds a crunchy texture and tropical flair to the dish.

TERIYAKI FISH AND SCALLION CAKES

For a unique and flavorful experience, try the Teriyaki Fish and Scallion Cakes. The tender fish is seasoned with a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce and paired with scallion cakes that add a light and refreshing touch.

SPICY AND STICKY CRISPY DRUMSTICKS

If you're in the mood for something a little spicy, the Spicy and Sticky Crispy Wings are sure to satisfy. The crispy wings are coated in a thick and spicy sauce that will leave your taste buds tingling.

GARLIC AND HERB STICKS

If you're in the mood for something simple yet flavorful, the Garlic and Herb Sticks are a perfect choice. The warm and aromatic sticks are brushed with a savory garlic and herb-infused butter that will have you coming back for more.

ORIENTAL STUFFED CHEESY MEATBALLS

For a fusion of flavors, try the Oriental Stuffed Cheesy Meatballs. The tender meatballs are stuffed with an Asian-inspired blend of spices and paired with a creamy and cheesy filling that adds magnificent flavors to the dish.

DELUXE

Savor the ultimate deluxe cuisine experience with Chef Ayoola's gourmet delights. Every dish is a work of art, meticulously crafted with the choicest ingredients and prepared to perfection to achieve a flawless balance of flavors.

PAD SEE EW

The Pad See Ew is a savory and satisfying Thai dish made with thick, flat rice noodles stir-fried with vegetables, soy sauce, and your choice of meat. The dish is bursting with umami flavor and a hint of sweetness.

TOM YUM GOONG

This popular Thai soup is made with fragrant lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal, with juicy shrimp and mushrooms added for a delicious twist. The soup is finished with a squeeze of lime juice for a refreshing tangy finish.

CREAMY CALAMARI AND PRAWN LINGUINE

The Creamy Calamari and Prawn Linguine is a must-try dish. The creamy linguine pasta is cooked to perfection and served with tender calamari and juicy prawns, creating a perfect blend of flavors and textures. The dish is finished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor.

STUFFED RAVIOLI WITH BASIL BUTTER IN TOMATO SAUCE

For a taste of Italy, try the Stuffed Ravioli with Basil Butter in Tomato Sauce. This dish features delicate pasta stuffed with a savory cheese filling, served with a flavorful tomato sauce and a drizzle of basil butter for a rich and satisfying meal that will transport your taste buds to the heart of Italy.

AYSUBZ MEATBALL MAMA MIA

Enjoy the AYSUBZ Meatball Mama Mia sandwich - a flavor-packed sandwich with juicy meatballs, tangy marinara sauce, and melted cheese on freshly baked bread. Every bite is a delicious explosion of flavors and textures, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal on the go.

DESSERTS

Indulge in a sweet and satisfying ending to your meal with these delectable desserts from Chef Ayoola's kitchen. Each dish is crafted with the finest ingredients and creative flair to provide a memorable dining experience.

CRÈME BRÛLÉE WITH RASPBERRY COOLIE AND DRIED CRANBERRIES

The Crème Brûlée with Raspberry Coulis and Dried Cranberries is a classic dessert elevated to the next level. The silky smooth custard is topped with a perfectly caramelized sugar crust, and served with a tangy raspberry coulis and chewy dried cranberries for a delicious contrast of flavors and textures.

CHOCOLATE CREPES WITH A DRIZZLE OF FRENCH VANILLA ICE CREAM

For a decadent and indulgent treat, try the Chocolate Crepes with a Drizzle of French Vanilla Ice Cream. The thin, delicate crepes are filled with rich chocolate and drizzled with a creamy French vanilla ice cream for a luxurious finish to your meal.

DECONSTRUCTED CRUMBLE PAIRED WITH A MANGO SORBET

The Deconstructed Crumble paired with a Mango Sorbet is a modern twist on a traditional dessert. The crumble is served in a deconstructed form, with a buttery crumble topping, sweet fruit filling, and a refreshing mango sorbet to cleanse your palate.

WARM CHOCOLATE BROWNIES & VANILLA ICE CREAM

If you're a fan of warm and gooey desserts, the Warm Chocolate Brownies & Vanilla Ice Cream is sure to satisfy. The rich, fudgy brownies are served warm and paired with a generous scoop of creamy vanilla ice cream, creating a perfect balance of sweet and indulgent.

STRAWBERRY ICE CREAM MIXED WITH SHORTBREAD CRUMBLE AND NUTS TOPPING

For a refreshing and crunchy dessert, try the Strawberry Ice Cream Mixed with Shortbread Crumble and Nuts Topping. The creamy strawberry ice cream is topped with a crunchy shortbread crumble and nut topping, providing a perfect mix of sweet and nutty flavors.
