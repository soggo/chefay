Meet Chef Ayoola

Chef Ayoola, a culinary virtuosa whose passion for creating sensational dishes is matched only by her unwavering commitment to culinary excellence.

Chef Ayoola attended Le Cordon Bleu Dusit in Bangkok, Thailand, where she received formal culinary training. She then gained practical experience interning at HSE Gourmet in Lagos, Nigeria, refining her skills and techniques as a chef.
With over 20 years of culinary experience under her belt, Chef Ayoola runs her own catering business, Eat Fresh and Events (AYSUBZ), and featured as the resident chef on TVC: Wake up Nigeria breakfast show, sharing her culinary expertise and recipes with millions of viewers across the nation.
